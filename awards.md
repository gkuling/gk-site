## Honors and Awards

  *Harvard Medical School, 2024–present*  
  Postdoctoral fellowship awarded by the Department of Biomedical Informatics to support curriculum development, educational research, and teaching in AI and medicine.

- **Medical Education Day Abstract Award**  
  *Harvard Medical School, Boston, MA — October 2024*  
  *Abstract title:* “Development of a Generative Artificial Intelligence Grading and Learning Tool.”  
  Recognized for innovation in AI-driven education and assessment.

- **Schmidt Science Fellowship Nominee**  
  *University of Toronto, 2023–2024*  
  Nominated as an international fellowship applicant recognizing innovative research contributions in AI and medical imaging.

  *University of Toronto, 2018–2023*  
  Annual scholarship supporting graduate research in science and technology.

- **Vector Institute Deep Learning and Reinforcement Learning Summer School**

  Selected participant in an intensive program focused on advanced deep learning and reinforcement learning applications.

- **First Place – Fields Thesis Competition**
